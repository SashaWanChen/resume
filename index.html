Introduction

My name is Chen, Wan- Chen. I am living in Tainan. In 2016，I am majoring in Industrial and Information Management in my college life.

Junior High School Phase

When I was a junior high student, I was ever elected as a member of the Exchange Program, in which we had to exchange and share habits, cultures, and environment with those from the Melrose High School in Australia. At the beginning, though we found that there is a huge gap between us. Yet somehow we can respect the differences, and then they could also adapt themselves to the life here. Being a responsible partner, I helped not only my own partner with everything in our school but also the others in any events. Through this experience, I have widened my horizons and realize that to make an activities wonderful, each and every member should be responsible, accommodating, positive and active.

High School Phase

I ever served for many activities held by my class. I believe that doing the best in any situation and knowing how to make the most of everyone are the ways to achieving success for a group.

In addition, I was a member of TNSP, accounting for holding many activities in our school. Working for every participation is also what we had to do. As class clothes are released, hunger free (DIY famine) done, happiness filled my heart and mind.

College Life

Freshman

I started to join MUN(Model United Nations) in my first semester. Pan Asia Mun was my first conference in my life. Though it has past about half a year, this special experience really gave a lesson to me. The more research I have done before the conference; the more chances I can think up ideas to solve the thorny problem with other delegates.

Also, I started to recognize that I can do something for our society by programming after entering this department. With this target, I have learned C and Python in the first semester with enthusiasm. In the second semester, I started to learn Java. And I used Android Studio to learn more application about Java.

Sophomore

Because I wish myself to organize an A Capella team in the near future, I joined in NCKU chorus after I stopped my chorus life from 2006 to 2012. And I change to use C++ as my major programming language. In the same time, I learn HTML5, CSS3, Dreamweaver in the course:視窗軟體與設計.
